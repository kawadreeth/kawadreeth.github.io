Bridge Optimization

11/2023
Modeled a 2D truss and estimated the force acting on each member, through parametric modeling on MATLAB, to determine the optimal geometry of the truss and validate our assumptions with testing. Testing methods involve compression using a press and using a load cell.

Brain Controlled Drone

Making a wearable headset and a drone, with a team of 6, using ADS1299. The drone will be built with off-the-shelf parts, and customized to the use case. The headset is 3D printed out of TPU, replicating the 10-20 system to hold the electrodes

Yaw Probe for a FSAE car

Designing a yaw probe to validate simulate data on how wind affects the yaw moment of the car. It has a precise adjustability of +- 10 degrees. It works on the principle of a pitot tube.
A related project is a toe alignment tool for the car which allows anyone to adjust the toe of each tire +- 3 degrees.

Automated Monopoly board

10/2023 - 4/2024
Designing the mechanical and controls system, with a team of 6, for an automated robotic monopoly game to enable human vs bot. The board is controlled using a stepper motor, slip ring and lever. The lever is supported along the walls using a bearing.

Strandbeest

3/2024
This is a 3D-printed mini Strandbeest, meticulously crafted on SolidWorks. Inspired by Theo Jansen's kinetic sculptures, it utilizes the Jansen Mechanism and mechanical gears to replicate captivating motion. The strandbeest is special for running purely on wind, and keeping the head completely stable.
